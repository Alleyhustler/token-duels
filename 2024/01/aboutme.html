Hello there ! It's Shye here, the creator of this website. I am a 36 year old human being from Finland, Northern part of Europe. Online I go by the names Shye, Shyebell and Itinerae. I'm currently unemployed, so I have plenty of time to code these stupid websites. :')

I'm a mother of two adorable cats. I am an only child and both of my parents are dead. Luckily I have a few amazing friends and a wonderful boyfriend.

I enjoy being outdoors, photographing nature, birdwatching, hiking long distances, adventuring and traveling, crafting website templates, listening music and going to concerts.

I am very shy and quiet, sometimes suffering from social anxiety and depression.

In the future I would like to work/travel around the world. (◠‿◠✿)
